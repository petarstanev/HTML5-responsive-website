1.Home

Telish Dental centre is one of the biggest dental clinics in the region.
It was made in 2013 with the idea to bring dental care to all the villages nearby to Telish.
The whole building is brand new and it is located opposite of the community building.
There are working 6 people. Three dental specialists with more than 15 years of working experience. 
One learning doctors who just finished university and two nurses.
